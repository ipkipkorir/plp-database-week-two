An attribute is a single piece of data of a specific datatype and it belongs to an entity in the database. it is a field or column of an entity.

A single-value attribute is one that accepts only one data value, for example the field for PRICE of an item.
A multi-valued attribute is one that accepts more that one value into one field, for example the field for storing CUSTOMER'S COMMENTS.
